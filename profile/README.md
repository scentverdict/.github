# ScentVerdict

**The UK's Which? for perfume.**

Independent, verdict-driven perfume reviews powered by AI. No fake reviews, no pay-to-play brand placements - just honest opinions on whether a fragrance is worth your money.

## What We Do

- **Independent Verdicts** - every perfume gets an editorial verdict: Favourite, Statement, or Acquired
- **AI Dupe Matching** - find affordable alternatives to designer fragrances using scent note analysis
- **ScentArt** - unique AI-generated visualisations that bring each fragrance to life on screen
- **UK Prices** - side-by-side pricing from trusted UK retailers with real price-per-ml breakdowns
- **Perfume Comparisons** - detailed head-to-head comparisons to help you choose
